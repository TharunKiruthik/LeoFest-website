CINIPHILIA


Description


Cinephillia is an event designed to test participants' knowledge and passion for movies. Through three rounds of challenges, teams will compete by identifying movie names, songs, and dialogues.


ROUNDS

Round 1: Name that Movie - Each team will be presented with a series of movie scenes, posters, or still images. - Teams must identify the name of the movie within a specified time limit. - Points will be awarded for each correct answer.

Round 2: Guess the Song - Teams will listen to short audio clips from various movie soundtracks. - Teams must identify the name of the song and the movie it belongs to within a specified time limit. - Points will be awarded for each correct answer.

Round 3: Dialogue Identification - Teams will be played short audio clips containing famous dialogues from movies. - Teams must identify the movie and the character who spoke the dialogue within a specified time limit. - Points will be awarded for each correct answer. Scoring: - Points will be awarded based on the accuracy and speed of the teams' responses in each round. - The team with the highest overall score at the end of

Finally declared the winner.
