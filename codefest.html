CODE FEST


Description


Experience the thrill of coding at our Coding Fest! Dive into a world where innovation meets technology. Join us for exhilarating challenges, hackathons, and workshops designed for all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a curious beginner, there's something for everyone. Engage in collaborative problem-solving, learn new skills, and push the boundaries of possibility. Don't miss out on this dynamic celebration of coding excellence!.


Rules and Regulations


1. There will be 2 rounds where a participant will move to 2nd round after the qualification.
2.Time limits will be strictly enforced for each round.
3.Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances
4.Only registered participants are allowed to compete in the coding fest.
5. Respectful and professional behavior is expected from all participants.
