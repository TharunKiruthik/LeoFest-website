DESIGN FEST


Description


Welcome to the Design Fest, where creativity meets functionality in UI/UX design! Participants will tackle real-world problems by crafting innovative designs using Figma. Showcase your skills in visual clarity, color theory, and design system implementation to solve the given problem statement.


Rules and Regulations


1. This is an individual competition focused on UI/UX design using Figma.
2. Participants will receive the problem statement on the event day and have a designated time to complete their design.
3. Designs must address the provided problem statement and adhere to the principles of good UI/UX design.
4. Judging criteria include neatness, clarity of presentation, effective use of colors, and implementation of design systems.
5. Plagiarism or use of pre-made templates will result in disqualification
