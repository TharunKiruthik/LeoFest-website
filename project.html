PROJECT PRESENTATION


Description


Embark on an exhilarating journey of innovation and collaboration at our Project Presentation! Immerse yourself in the excitement of showcasing your projects, presenting groundbreaking ideas, and engaging with a dynamic audience. Seize this opportunity to demonstrate your expertise, articulate your vision & get recognized in an atmosphere with like-minded individuals. Don't miss out the chance to elevate your project to new heights and celebrate the spirit of innovation at our Project Presentation @ Leo Fest 24


Rules and Regulations


1. Each project presentation team must consist of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 3 members (including team leader).
2. The team leader must register for the event to participate in this competition. Only registered students are allowed to participate in this event.
3. Each project presentation will be allocated a specific time limit of 5 to 10 minutes for the presentation.
4. Presenters should take precautions to secure their equipment and valuables (slides, videos, components, etc,).
5. Participants should showcase your hardware or software projects and clearly explain them to the reviewer.
