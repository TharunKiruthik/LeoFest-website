SKETCH THE ART


Description


Don't miss your chance to be a part of this extraordinary event, where art ,colour,creativity & innovation collide to create a truly unforgettable & colourful experience. Register now for LEO FEST's Artistic Showcase and let your creativity soar colourfully!


Rules and Regulations


1. The competition is open to all currently enrolled students at the college, both registered beforehand and those who register on the spot before the competition begins
2.On the day of the competition, participants will be provided with on-spot topics or prompts to inspire their pencil artwork. These topics will be revealed at the start of the competition, allowing participants to showcase their creativity and adaptability.
3.Only pencil art is allowed for this competition. Participants must use graphite pencils or charcoal pencils for their creations
4. Participants have one hour to complete their pencil artwork from start to finish.
5. Participants must submit their finished pencil artwork to the organizers before the time limit expires.
6. Participants must bring their own pencils and sharpener to ensure that they maintain a sharp pencil point throughout the competition.
