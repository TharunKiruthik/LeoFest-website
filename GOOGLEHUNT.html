GOOGLE HUNT


Description


Are you ready to involve yourself in a dynamic event “GOOGLE HUNT” which challenges your search and problem-solving skills? In the first, participants seek hidden logos using Google search, enhancing search skills. The second round challenges problem-solving abilities through riddles. This unique competition blends digital prowess with mental agility, offering an exciting and educational experience for participants.


Rules and Regulations


1. Round 1 - Logo Hunt:

1.Teams or individual must use Google to search and identify logos based on provided clues.
2.A time limit of 15 minutes per logo search to encourage swift and strategic decisionmaking.
3. Each correctly identified logo earns points, with bonus points for speed.

2. Round 2 - Riddle Solving:

1.Team or individual who qualifies in the first round can only participate in this round
2.Teams face interactive riddles presented in an engaging format.
3.A time limit of 20 minutes for each riddle, promoting quick thinking and effective communication within the team.
4.Correctly solving a riddle earns points, with escalating difficulty for subsequent challenges.
