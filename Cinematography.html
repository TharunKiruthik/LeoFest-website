ACTION & CUT


Description


Be ready to unleash your imagination and share your unique stories as motion picture in Our Big Stage. “ACTION & CUT” is the perfect platform for emerging filmmakers , storytellers and Cinematographers around us where the budding filmmakers showcase their talent and creativity through their short film.


Rules and Regulations


1. Short films, Documentaries, The Essence of Nature and Music Video are purely encouraged with Minimum Duration of 3 Minutes to 30 Minutes.
2.Each presentation team will get 5 min for presentation + 03 min for Q & A.
3.The presentation should be only in MS PowerPoint (ppt /pptx).
4.Maximum number of 12 slides only allowed and must be in Times New Roman font completely.
5. Plagiarism is not entertained.
